Passive Desalinator
This is a project inspired by a simple emergency water accumulation technique to help areas in the world without power remove the salt from their water sources.

It would work using solar energy reflected on several chambers using the physics of evaporating water to ensure the salt is left behind.

History Timeline
This is just a working model of an idea for a easy-to-use timeline of all of history. The backend database for this project is currently under development. The premise is for a region comparable view of the dynasties of history with important events captured overtop these dyntasties. Eventually, I would like to add a map alongside the timeline so that the territorial view of the world can augment the timeline information.

Lifestyle Budget
Most people budget enough money to get through the next day, week, month, maybe year, but what about lifetime? The idea behind this project is to create a visual aid to guide the filling of “buckets” like “retirement” or “college”. After inputing your future plans and expected amount cost of adventures, you can input an income level to see how sustainable that lifestyle is for you.
